# Privacy Policy for Hearo

**Last updated:** January 29, 2026

## Overview

Hearo is an ear training application that helps users develop their musical hearing skills through interactive exercises and quizzes.

## Data Collection

Hearo does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. All app data, including your progress and settings, is stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services

Hearo does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Permissions

Hearo does not require any special device permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location access.

## Children's Privacy

Hearo does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/bilicidevOrg/Hearo).
